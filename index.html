About Us

What Have We Experienced

Since 2002, Fish Shop Pearcedale has been a beloved family-run spot in Pearcedale, serving up classic fish and chips with a touch of tradition. Rooted in our family's passion for timeless recipes, we've stayed true to what works—simple, satisfying meals that bring back fond memories with every taste.

Our signature Flake is a crowd favorite: perfectly crispy on the outside, tender and flaky inside, cooked to perfection in fresh vegetable oils. Quality is our priority—we source local produce whenever possible, and every order is prepared fresh daily, ensuring that each bite is as delicious as the last.
